# One Piece
In order to become the next King of the Pirates, Monkey D. Luffy, a young man whose body turned rubbery after consuming a Devil Fruit, and his group, the Straw Hat Pirates, must find the fabled "One Piece" treasure. The story follows their adventures across a fantastical world filled with diverse islands, epic battles, and clashes between pirates, the Marines, and a powerful World Government. Luffy's ultimate goal is to discover the One Piece riches left by the previous Pirate King, Gold Roger, and take the title for himself. 

Straw Hat Pirates (Main Crew).
Monkey D. Luffy - Captain with aspirations of becoming the Pirate King.
Roronoa Zoro - Swordsman; aspires to be the world's best swordsman.
Nami is a navigator with expertise in cartography and weather.
Usopp is a great sniper who also tells stories.
Sanji - Cook; master cook and martial artist who practices the "Black Leg" method.
Tony Tony Chopper - Doctor, a talking reindeer that consumed the Human-Human Fruit.
Nico Robin, an archaeologist, can sprout bodily parts utilizing the Flower-Flower Fruit.
Franky - Shipwright, a cyborg who built the Thousand Sunny.
Brook is a musician, a living skeleton revived by the Revive-Revive fruit.
Jinbe - Helmsman; a fisherman and former Sun Pirates member.

# Dr. Stone
A brilliant high school student named Senku must use his scientific skills to reconstruct civilization after a mysterious light transforms all of humanity to stone in the manga and anime series Dr. Stone. Senku awakens after 3,700 years and begins to resurrect humanity, sparking conflict with those who prefer to reconstruct civilization via "might" rather than science. The novel is praised for its scientific accuracy and celebration of human development, which combine adventure, comedy, and scientific instruction. 

Kingdom of Science (Main Group).
Senku Ishigami - The primary character; a brilliant scientist who wishes to recreate humanity through science.
Taiju Oki - Senku's best friend; strong, diligent, and loyal, but not particularly bright.
Taiju's love interest, Yuzuriha Ogawa, is adept in crafts and sewing.
Chrome - A self-taught "sorcerer" who serves as Senku's apprentice scientist.
Kohaku - A valiant warrior girl who assists Senku and becomes one of his most trusted companions.
Gen Asagiri, a mentalist (illusionist and psychologist), joins the Kingdom of Science.
Kaseki - An elderly craftsman with extensive experience manufacturing and fabricating tools.
Suika is a lovely, curious little girl who wears a watermelon on her head and excels at observing and snooping around.
