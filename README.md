# One Piece
In order to become the next King of the Pirates, Monkey D. Luffy, a young man whose body turned rubbery after consuming a Devil Fruit, and his group, the Straw Hat Pirates, must find the fabled "One Piece" treasure. The story follows their adventures across a fantastical world filled with diverse islands, epic battles, and clashes between pirates, the Marines, and a powerful World Government. Luffy's ultimate goal is to discover the One Piece riches left by the previous Pirate King, Gold Roger, and take the title for himself. 


# Dr. Stone
A brilliant high school student named Senku must use his scientific skills to reconstruct civilization after a mysterious light transforms all of humanity to stone in the manga and anime series Dr. Stone. Senku awakens after 3,700 years and begins to resurrect humanity, sparking conflict with those who prefer to reconstruct civilization via "might" rather than science. The novel is praised for its scientific accuracy and celebration of human development, which combine adventure, comedy, and scientific instruction.
